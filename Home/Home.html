About Heartfulness

Heartfulness is a worldwide spiritual organization helping humanity to experience peace of mind, develop mental faculties and lead a meaningful life. We offer Heartfulness meditation practice to all men and women, without any distinction of nationality, religion, or caste.

Our Meditation System

Ancient Roots, Modern Practice

This system is evolved from the ancient tradition of Raja Yoga and uses the spiritual technique of 'Pranahuti' or 'Yogic Transmission'. This unique approach enables us to experience our true divine nature in our hearts.

Simple and Effective

The Heartfulness practice is simple and effective and can be done in the comfort of your own home. No special equipment or complex techniques are required.

Benefits of Regular Practice

As we meditate regularly, we experience:

  • Internal peace and calmness of mind
  • Progress towards a balanced state of existence
  • Freedom from mental pressures and anxieties
  • Significant contribution to spiritual, mental and physical well-being
