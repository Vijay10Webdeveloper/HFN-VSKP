About Heartfulness Yoga

We have a meditative approach to Yoga that allows us to explore the physical, mental and spiritual all at once.

Heartfulness Yoga brings you this full spectrum of Yoga by creating a seamless flow between asanas and meditation in every class. The uniqueness of Yogic Transmission is at the heart of our practice; each programme exposes you to the exclusive experience of the Heartfulness Practices, all designed to carry you deeper in your journey.
